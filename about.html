About Us

Our Mission

Project Outreach is meant to encourage student engagement and activism. In this club, we will cultivate next-generation thinking and take action in bettering the community. Our mission is to connect students with engaging and immersive service opportunities, but also to unite and motivate Trevor's volunteer community. As a group, we will paint schools, work at soup kitchens, food banks, fundraising events, and any other volunteer opportunities you are interested in. Some specific organizations that I have contacted for our cause are Habitat for Humanity, St. Joe’s Soup Kitchen, Holy Apostles Soup Kitchen, God’s Love We Deliver, and City Meals on Wheels. Several times a semester we will participate in hands-on work at these kinds of locations, while also reflecting on further steps that can be taken to reach out to those in need.
